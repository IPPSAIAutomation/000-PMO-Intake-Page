üìä Scoring Guide

AI Strategy Score (1-10)

Measures alignment with organizational strategic priorities and long-term goals.

9-10: Critical strategic alignment, transformational impact
7-8: Strong strategic value, significant advancement
5-6: Moderate strategic benefit, supports key initiatives
3-4: Minor strategic value, tangential alignment
1-2: Minimal strategic impact, operational only

AI Impact Score (1-10)

Quantifies the expected business impact and value delivered.

9-10: Enterprise-wide impact, major efficiency gains
7-8: Department-level impact, measurable improvements
5-6: Team-level impact, noticeable benefits
3-4: Individual-level impact, incremental value
1-2: Minimal impact, limited scope

Tech Effort (1-3)

Estimates the technical complexity and resource requirements.

1 (Low): Simple implementation, existing tools, <1 week
2 (Medium): Moderate complexity, some custom work, 1-4 weeks
3 (High): Complex integration, significant development, >4 weeks

Final Prioritization Score

Final Score = (AI Strategy Score + AI Impact Score) / Tech Effort

Higher scores indicate higher priority projects that deliver maximum value with optimal resource investment.
